Ol (Otus Lisp) is a purely* functional dialect of [derived from::Lisp](https://rosettacode.org/wiki/derived_from::Lisp).

It implements an extended subset of [R<sup>5</sup>RS](http://www.schemers.org/Documents/Standards/R5RS/) ([R<sup>7</sup>RS](http://www.r7rs.org) from version 2.0) Scheme including, but not limited to, some of the [SRFI](http://srfi.schemers.org/)s, open source / free software, with a permissive license.

It is small (approx 42kb virtual machine size), embeddable and crossplatform (x86/64, aarch64, arm, powerpc, mips32/64; GNU/Linux, Windows, Unix and *BSDs, Android, webOS, Minoca, Odroid, LattePanda; Web in asm.js form).

Builds and source codes can be found at project [home page](https://yuriy-chumak.github.io/ol/) or [Github](https://github.com/yuriy-chumak/ol).
