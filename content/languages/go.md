'''Go''' is an open source project developed by a team at [Google](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Google) (including [Robert Griesemer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Robert_Griesemer), [Rob Pike](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Rob_Pike) and [Ken Thompson](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Ken_Thompson)) as well as many [contributors](http://golang.org/CONTRIBUTORS) from the open source community.

Go is distributed under a [BSD-style license](http://golang.org/LICENSE).

Not to be confused with [Go!](https://rosettacode.org/wiki/:Category:Go!)
