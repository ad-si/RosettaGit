GDC is the D compiler based on the DMD front end and the [GCC](https://rosettacode.org/wiki/GCC) backend. It is buildable on GCC 3.4.6, GCC 4.1.2 and GCC 4.3.5 and comes with version 1.0.62 of the front end.
